Strawberry Juice

Prep Time: 10 Mins

Cook Time: No Cook

Serves:2

Ingredients

  • Strawberries 1 cup
  • Lime juice 1 tbsp
  • Sugar 3-4 tbsp
  • Salt 3-4 tbsp
  • Iced Water 1-1/2 cup
  • Ice cube 5-6

Cooking Directions

  1. Wash, hull and cut up strawberries.
